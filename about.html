About This Website

Welcome to TypingTest — your interactive platform for practicing and improving your typing skills. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone who loves to type, this tool helps you boost your speed and accuracy through real-time feedback.

Built using clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, TypingTest is lightweight and responsive across devices. The test features a timer, real-time word count, WPM (words per minute), and accuracy tracking to help you monitor your progress.

Typing is more than just speed — it's about precision, rhythm, and muscle memory. With this platform, you can practice smarter and more efficiently.
